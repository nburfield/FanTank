Summary of Service

Fantank is an electronic platform where qualified investors can view private equity and debt deals. We refer to each these deals as a “Campaign.” For example, an Campaign might consist of a production company (referred to as an "Issuer") raising private funds for a stage show, in exchange for an equity share of the company where investors receive a return on their investment (for example, a share of the profits). Each Campaign is held by a separate Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) that is a limited liability company or limited partnership or other corporate entity.

In the case of debt Campaigns, an investor who wants to invest would make a loan to the SPV holding the Underlying Loan. The investor’s loan is evidenced by a promissory note, which we refer to as the “Note.” The Note gives the investor the right to receive payments of interest and principal, only to the extent that the SPV receives payments from the Issuer with respect to the Underlying Loan.

For a more detailed description, you should review the FAQ section of the Site and to the information provided by each Campaign.
